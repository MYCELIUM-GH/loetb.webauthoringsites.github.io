Dublin, 18 October 2024 - The Apex Legends2024 Championship concluded yesterday with a thrilling match between the top two teams, Phantom Squad1 and Echo Raiders2. The audience was on the edge of their seats as Phantom Squad1 clinched the victory in the final moments of the game.

Record-Breaking Viewership

The tournament set new records with over 106 (1 million) viewers tuning in from around the globe. Fans were treated to a display of unparalleled skill and strategy.

MVP of the Tournament

The MVP award went to ShadowStrike1, the ace player from Phantom Squad1, who delivered a series of impressive plays that left the audience in awe. His K/D ratio of 41/21 was the highest in the entire tournament.
